🎮 How to Play

Simple to learn, hard to master

1

Observe the Grid

Study the grid of colored arrows pointing Up, Down, Left and Right.

2

Tap an Arrow

Tapping an arrow makes it fly forward in the direction it points.

3

Keep the Path Clear

If another arrow blocks the path, it vibrates and stays put — collision!

4

Clear & Earn Stars

Find the perfect order to release every arrow and escape the grid.
