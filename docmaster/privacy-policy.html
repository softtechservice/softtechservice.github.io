Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

This data collection is handled by Google AdMob in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

üì± Permissions Usage

The app requests permissions only to provide core functionality:

Permission Purpose
Camera Scan documents using your device camera. Images are processed locally and stored only on your device.
Storage / Photos Save scanned documents, PDFs, and images. Access images from gallery for scanning or editing. Required for document management features.
Internet Display advertisements via Google AdMob. Not required for core app functionality.

Note: The app does NOT require READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permission on Android 13+ as it uses scoped storage APIs (file_picker, image_picker) which comply with Google Play's Photo and Video Permissions policy.

üîê Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All documents, PDFs, images, extracted text, user profile information (name, avatar), and app preferences are stored locally on your device using SQLite and Hive databases
  • Profile Management: User profile information is managed locally within the app. Your name and avatar are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to external servers
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync, backup, or upload your documents, profile information, or any other data to any cloud service
  • No External Servers: Your documents, profile information, and app data never leave your device
  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use analytics SDKs to track your app usage
  • Data Deletion: You can delete any document, PDF, extracted text, or profile information at any time from within the app. Clearing app data will remove all stored information including your profile
