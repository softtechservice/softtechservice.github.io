✨ Key Features

Everything you need to confidently pass your RTO exam

Exam Simulator

Mirrors the exact RTO test format to prepare you for the real deal.

  • 20 random questions
  • 20-minute countdown timer
  • Jump to any question with grid sheet
  • 60% passing threshold (12/20 to pass)
  • Share your result score-card image

Practice Quiz

Systematic practice of the complete question bank.

  • Instant right/wrong feedback
  • Progress tracked per question
  • Resumes where you left off
  • All official question types

Multilingual Support

Study efficiently in your preferred Indian language natively.

  • English
  • Hindi (हिन्दी)
  • Marathi (मराठी)
  • Gujarati (ગુજરાતી)
  • Tamil (தமிழ்)

Traffic Signs Library

Visual learning tool for all official Indian traffic signs.

  • 160 official traffic sign images
  • Search by sign name
  • Filter by mandatory/cautionary/informatory
  • Fast visual grid presentation

State Codes & Fees

Crucial RTO information updated and easily accessible.

  • Codes for 35 States & UTs (Search & Copy)
  • Complete organized fee table structure
  • Fees for Licence, Registration & Permit
  • Highlight of important surcharge notes

RTO Documents & Forms

A complete reference guide, allowing downloads of official CMVR forms.

  • DL document requirement checklists
  • Download 22 official PDF forms
  • Applicable across India (CMVR 1989)
  • Open seamlessly in device viewer

📊 Track Your Progress

Stay motivated with a beautiful Progress Dashboard. Monitor practiced vs. total questions, overall accuracy percentage, daily streaks, and review your recent mock test history directly on your home screen.
