1. Protocol Overview

Welcome to GameHub, an offline game console application developed by SoftTech Service. Your privacy is our top priority. GameHub is designed as an Offline-First application, meaning your data stays where it belongs: on your device.

2. Data Collection Log

A. Personal Identity Data

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). No names, emails, or phone numbers are required to access the console.

B. Local Environment Data

The following data is stored locally on your device to ensure persistence:

  • Mission High Scores & Achievement Logs.
  • User Configuration (Sound, Theme, Haptic Feedback).
  • Session Metadata (used for in-app review triggers).

This data is encrypted within the app's local storage and is never uploaded to external servers.

3. External Uplinks

GameHub utilizes limited third-party services to support the application ecosystem:

A. Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to serve relevant content. This is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

B. System Review API

We utilize the Google Play In-App Review API. All interactions with this API are handled by Google Play Services.

4. Hardware Permissions

  • Network Access: Required only for ad synchronization and store updates.
  • Haptics: Used for tactile feedback during gameplay (User-configurable).
