Color Sort – Water Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by SoftTech Service and respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our Android application.

Short version: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All game data stays on your device. No account is required to play.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress — completed levels, current level, and scores
  • Daily check-in streak and coin balance
  • Settings preferences (sound, display options)

Important: All data is stored locally using Flutter's shared preferences and local storage. Nothing is sent to our servers or any third-party server.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, etc.)
  • Location or GPS data
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal files

Permissions Usage

The app requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

Permission Purpose Required?
Internet Load rewarded ads (optional), Google Fonts Optional
Storage (Read) Save/load game progress locally Core
Vibration Haptic feedback during gameplay Optional

The game can be played completely offline. Internet is only used when you choose to watch a rewarded video ad for bonus coins.

Advertising

Color Sort – Water Puzzle includes optional advertising to support free access to the game:

Rewarded Video Ads

  • Completely optional — you choose to watch ads to earn bonus coins
  • Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may use advertising ID to serve relevant ads
  • You may opt out of personalized ads through your Android device settings

Banner Ads

  • Non-intrusive banners may appear in certain screens
  • Available as a "Remove Ads" in-app purchase for an ad-free experience

AdMob Privacy: Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to serve personalized ads. Visit Google's Privacy Policy to learn more or opt out.

Data Storage & Security

  • All game progress is stored locally on your device
  • No cloud sync or external server storage
  • No third-party analytics SDKs or tracking libraries
  • No user accounts or login required
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all local data

Children's Privacy

Color Sort – Water Puzzle is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. No registration, login, or personal information is required to play.

The game complies with Google Play's Families Policy. Ad content is filtered to be appropriate for all audiences.

Internet Usage

The app is fully functional offline. Internet connectivity is used only when:

  • You choose to watch a rewarded video ad to earn bonus coins
  • Loading Google Fonts for consistent typography (cached after first load)

Core gameplay, level progression, and all puzzle content works without an internet connection.

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
