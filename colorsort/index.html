How to Play

Easy to Learn.
Hard to Put Down.

Four simple steps to puzzle perfection. No tutorial needed — just tap and sort!

01

Select a Tube

Tap any test tube to select it. The topmost color layer will be highlighted and ready to pour.

02

Pour the Color

Tap a second tube to pour. Colors must match or the tube must be empty. Smart rules, satisfying results.

03

Keep Sorting

Continue pouring until every tube holds a single, solid color from bottom to top.

04

Win the Level!

All sorted? Score saved, next level unlocked. Progress through hundreds of handcrafted challenges!
