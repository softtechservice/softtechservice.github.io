Live IPO ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our Flutter application.

🔍 Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

  • Calculator inputs (for IPO, SIP, EMI calculations, etc.)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)

Important: All calculator data and preferences are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect or transmit this data to any servers.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Firebase Analytics:

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Screen views and navigation flows
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Crash reports and error logs (Firebase Crashlytics)

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identity information
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Financial transaction data
  • Precise location data
  • Contact information

📱 Permissions Usage

The app requests minimal permissions:

Permission Purpose
Internet Fetch live IPO data, GMP updates, subscription information
Network State Monitor connectivity for cache management
Notifications Send IPO event alerts via Firebase Cloud Messaging (optional)

🔐 Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Calculator history and preferences stored using Hive database
  • Cache: IPO data cached locally for 10 minutes for offline access
  • Firebase: Anonymous analytics and crash reports only
  • No Cloud Sync: Your calculator data never leaves your device
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data

🌐 Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Services (Google)

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reporting and stability monitoring
  • Firebase Remote Config: Dynamic app configuration
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications for IPO events

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

IPO Data Sources

IPO data is fetched from public APIs. We do not control or guarantee the accuracy of this data.

🔄 Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal data.

Anonymous analytics data is shared with Firebase (Google) for app improvement purposes only.

👶 Children's Privacy

Live IPO does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
