⚠️ Important Notice

Internet Connection Required

Data Disclaimer

This app requires an active internet connection to fetch live IPO data, GMP updates, and subscription information. Cached data is available for offline viewing (10-minute cache), but fresh data requires connectivity.

About GMP Data: All GMP figures and IPO data are gathered from various public platforms and market trackers. These values may change frequently and may sometimes be incorrect or outdated.

Not Financial Advice: This app is not a financial advisory platform. Nothing displayed should be considered investment advice. Users should conduct their own research or consult a certified financial advisor.
