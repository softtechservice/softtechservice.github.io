Legal Information & Policies

Last updated: 2026-03-23 | Developer: SoftTech Service

UltraPlay 3D/VR ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our Android video player application.

🔍 Information We Process

1. Local Media Files

UltraPlay acts purely as a local media player. It indexes video and audio files stored on your device to display them in the app's library and allow playback. This indexing occurs entirely offline.

Important: We do not upload your media files, playback history, private folders, or PIN codes to any external servers. All personal data stays on your device.

2. Data Collected by Third Parties

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may use advertising identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID) and cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your Android device's Settings > Google > Ads.
  • OpenSubtitles API: If you use the in-app subtitle search feature, the app queries the OpenSubtitles API with the hash/filename of your video to find matching subtitles.

📱 Permissions Usage

The app requests the following core permissions to function properly:

Permission Purpose
Read/Write External Storage / Media Audio, Video To discover, organize, and play your local video and audio files.
Internet & Network State To play network streams (HLS/DASH/Direct URLs), search for subtitles online, and serve advertisements.
Foreground Service To allow continuous background playback and provide media controls in the notification drawer when you switch apps.
System Alert Window (Draw over other apps) To enable Picture-in-Picture (PiP) mode and mini-player functionality.
Wake Lock To prevent the screen from turning off while you are actively watching a video.

🔐 Private Folder & PIN

The Private Folder feature uses a 4-digit PIN to restrict access to videos within the app. Please note that this feature is an app-level privacy measure; the files themselves remain on your device's filesystem. We cannot recover forgotten PINs as they are securely hashed and stored only on your local Room database.

👶 Children's Privacy

UltraPlay 3D/VR does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal information from a visitor under the age of 13, we will delete the information from our records.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated revision date.
