1. Local Data Storage (No Server)

The app stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Personal information (name, contact details, date of birth, gender, marital status)
  • Family information (father's name, mother's name)
  • Professional information (education, work experience, skills, projects, certifications)
  • Resume and biodata content
  • ID card and business card data
  • Profile photos, signatures, and logos
  • AI-generated content preferences

All data is stored locally using Hive local storage. We do NOT have any servers. No data is transmitted to our servers or third parties. Everything remains on your device.

2. User Profile Management

User profiles are managed locally within the app. Each profile includes:

  • Address details (current and permanent)
  • Professional summary
  • Profile photo

Profile data is stored locally and never transmitted to external servers. We do not have access to your profile data.

We do NOT collect any data:

  • No personal information is collected by us
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we do NOT have any servers)
  • No cloud storage or backup servers
  • No analytics or tracking data
  • No device identifiers
  • No location data
  • No user behavior data
  • No resume or document content is collected

All data remains on your device and is under your complete control. We do NOT use any servers for data storage.
