🔍 Information Collection and Use

A. Personal Data

The Application does NOT require any user registration. We do NOT collect, store, or solicit any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number).

Important: No account, login, or personal information is required to play Tower Up!

B. Automatically Collected Data (Third-Party Services)

The Application uses third-party services that may automatically collect information used to identify your device. This information is handled by our third-party service providers according to their respective privacy policies:

1. Google AdMob (Google Play Services)

  • The Application integrates the Google AdMob SDK to serve advertising.
  • AdMob may collect and process device identifiers, advertising IDs (e.g., Android Advertising ID), coarse location data, IP address, and app interaction data.
  • This data is used to provide, personalize, and analyze advertising.
  • For more details, review how Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

2. Google Play Services (In-App Review & In-App Update)

  • The Application uses Google Play APIs to check for system updates and prompt in-app reviews.
  • These APIs may collect diagnostic and telemetry data to manage software delivery and store interactions.
  • Review the Google Privacy Policy.

💾 Local Storage and Preferences

The Application stores simple, non-personal configurations locally on your device via Android's SharedPreferences framework:

Stored Data Purpose
Game Settings Sound effects toggles and haptic feedback options
Game State Selected speed / difficulty level
Gameplay Statistics Local scoreboard lists (score values, dates, and ranks)

This data is kept locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers. You can erase this data at any time by selecting "Clear Scores" in the app's Scoreboard menu, or by clearing the Application's storage via your device settings.

👶 Children's Privacy

These services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can perform the necessary deletion actions.

🚫 Opt-Out Rights

You can easily stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it. Use the standard uninstall processes available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace.

Additionally, you can opt out of personalized advertising served by Google AdMob by managing your device settings:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Ads on your Android device and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or "Reset advertising ID".

🔄 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
