🎮 How to Play

Four simple steps to build to the heavens

1

Watch the Crane

The crane swings the building blocks back and forth above your tower.

2

Tap to Drop

Tap anywhere on the screen to release the block from the crane.

3

Align Perfectly

Stack directly on top. Uneven drops make your tower swing, shrink, and fall!

4

Build Combos

Perfect stacks trigger combos for higher scores. Reach for the sky!
